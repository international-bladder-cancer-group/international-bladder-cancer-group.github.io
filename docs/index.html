Mission Statement

The mission of the IBCG is two-pronged:

  1. To improve outcomes of patients suffering from bladder cancer through critical evaluation and leveraging of existing data to drive evidence-based treatment.
  2. To develop practical educational tools that are applicable to the global urological community and thus enhance the care of patients with bladder cancer on a global level.
