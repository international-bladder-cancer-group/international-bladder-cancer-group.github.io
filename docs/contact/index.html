Membership

IBCG membership is limited to clinicians, scientists, and patient advocates dedicated to advancing the IBCG mission to enhance the care of patients with bladder cancer on a global level. Members are expected to actively participate in all scheduled IBCG activities, including in-person attendance at the annual retreat.

New applicants for membership must be nominated by a current, active IBCG member in good standing.

Interested applicants should submit the following to the Board of Directors for consideration:

Email: members@ibcg.info
Subject Line: Application for IBCG Membership

  1. Your curriculum vitae
  2. Letter outlining why you desire to join IBCG membership
  3. A supporting letter from an active IBCG member in good standing
