International Bladder Cancer Group

Health Service Research

Lead by Drs. Stephen B. Williams & Yair Lotan, the Health Services Research (HSR) arm of the IBCG aims to be THE collaborative source for global clinicians and researchers, to improve population health of bladder cancer patients at large.
